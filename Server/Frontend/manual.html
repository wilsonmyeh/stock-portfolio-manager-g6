Stock Portfolio Manager User Manual

Introduction

The Stock Portfolio Manager is the web application to track, manage and analyze an investor's stock portfolio.

Getting Started

Open http://localhost/Frontend/login.html in a web browser and enter your username and password. If you forgot your password, click the "Forgot Password?" button, which will take you to an account recovery page. To recover you password, simply enter the email associated with you account into the field, click "Submit", and follow the prompts in following recovery emails. After a successful login, you will be taken to the dashboard screen, whose various features are explained below.
